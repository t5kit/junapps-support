Sales Tax Calculator

Sales Tax Calculator is a simple and fast app for calculating sales tax and the total price after tax. Enter a subtotal, choose a sales tax rate, and see the tax amount and total instantly.

🧾 App Description

Sales Tax Calculator is designed to make shopping and price checking easier. Quickly calculate the sales tax amount and final total with a clean and simple interface.

You can also save frequently used tax rates, such as your local sales tax rate or rates for places you often visit. Tap a saved tax rate to apply it instantly, or edit and delete saved rates when needed.

✨ Features

  • Calculate sales tax instantly
  • See the final total after tax
  • Enter any custom sales tax rate
  • Save frequently used tax rates
  • Tap a saved tax rate to apply it quickly
  • Edit or delete saved tax rates
  • Simple and clean design
  • Optional ad removal

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: Can I enter my own sales tax rate?
    A: Yes. You can enter any custom sales tax rate manually.
  • Q: Can I save tax rates I use often?
    A: Yes. You can save frequently used tax rates and apply them with a tap.
  • Q: Can I edit or delete saved tax rates?
    A: Yes. You can edit or delete saved tax rates from the saved rates list.
  • Q: Does the app show both tax and total?
    A: Yes. The app shows the sales tax amount and the final total after tax.
  • Q: Does the app support different currencies?
    A: Yes. The app uses your device’s current locale to display the appropriate currency.
  • Q: Can I remove ads?
    A: Yes. You can remove banner ads with an optional in-app purchase.
  • Q: Do I need an internet connection?
    A: The sales tax calculation itself works offline. An internet connection may be required to load ads or complete in-app purchases.
