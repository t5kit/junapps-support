📄 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application “Unit Price Calculator” provided by the developer.

1. Information We Collect

Unit Price Calculator does not require account registration and does not ask users to enter personal information.

The app allows users to enter item prices, quantities, and units in order to calculate unit prices and compare products. These values are used only for calculation within the app and are not sent to the developer.

The app does not collect, store, or share any item prices, quantities, units, calculation results, or usage data on external servers operated by the developer.

2. Local App Data

Unit Price Calculator may store simple app data locally on the user’s device, such as whether ads have been removed through an in-app purchase.

This local data is used only to provide app functionality and is not transmitted to the developer.

3. In-App Purchases

Unit Price Calculator offers optional in-app purchases, such as removing ads or supporting future updates.

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive or store users’ payment card information.

4. Advertising

Unit Price Calculator may display banner ads using Google AdMob.

Third-party advertising services may collect and use certain information, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, and ad interaction data, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Users may remove banner ads through an optional in-app purchase.

5. Third-Party Services

Unit Price Calculator uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob for displaying ads
  • Apple App Store for processing in-app purchases

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies and platform rules.

6. Children’s Privacy

Unit Price Calculator is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer so that appropriate action can be taken.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through the app or via the distribution platform.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the support information provided on the App Store.
