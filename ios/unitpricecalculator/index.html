Unit Price Calculator

Unit Price Calculator is a simple and fast app for comparing unit prices and finding the better deal. Enter the price, quantity, and unit for two items, and see which one offers better value instantly.

🛒 App Description

Unit Price Calculator is designed to make shopping and price comparison easier. Quickly compare products by unit price with a clean and simple interface.

You can enter each item’s price, quantity, and unit separately. The app automatically converts compatible units such as oz, lb, g, kg, ml, and L, then highlights the better deal.

It is useful for comparing groceries, drinks, household items, bulk packages, coffee, detergent, snacks, and more.

✨ Features

  • Compare two items side by side
  • Calculate unit prices instantly
  • Enter price, quantity, and unit for each item
  • Supports oz, lb, g, kg, ml, and L
  • Automatically converts compatible units
  • Highlights the better deal
  • Shows a clear notice when units cannot be compared
  • Simple and clean design
  • Optional ad removal

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: What is a unit price?
    A: A unit price is the price per unit of measurement, such as price per oz, lb, g, kg, ml, or L. It helps you compare products of different sizes more accurately.
  • Q: Can I compare two different package sizes?
    A: Yes. Enter the price and quantity for each item, and the app will calculate the unit price for both.
  • Q: Can I use different units for Item A and Item B?
    A: Yes. You can choose a different unit for each item. Compatible units are converted automatically.
  • Q: Which units are supported?
    A: The app supports oz, lb, g, kg, ml, and L.
  • Q: Can the app compare weight and volume?
    A: No. Weight units and volume units cannot be compared directly. The app shows a clear notice when the selected units are not comparable.
  • Q: Does the app show which item is cheaper?
    A: Yes. The app highlights the item with the better unit price as the best deal.
  • Q: Can I remove ads?
    A: Yes. You can remove banner ads with an optional in-app purchase.
  • Q: Do I need an internet connection?
    A: The unit price calculation itself works offline. An internet connection may be required to load ads or complete in-app purchases.
