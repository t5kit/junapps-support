Room Scan is a simple and intuitive room-scanning app designed for clarity and speed. Using your device‚Äôs LiDAR sensor, you can scan rooms and instantly view them in an interactive 3D model.

Walls, floors, doors, windows, and furniture are automatically detected, allowing you to understand the structure of a space at a glance. You can freely rotate, zoom, and inspect the scanned room, and tap individual elements to highlight them and view details.

All scanned rooms are saved locally and organized in a history list, making it easy to revisit past scans and manage multiple spaces. A sample room is included so you can explore all features before scanning your own environment.

The app is fully usable without a subscription. With the Silver plan, detailed dimensions for rooms, walls, floors, and objects become visible, making it easier to plan layouts, renovations, or furniture placement. For advanced workflows, the Gold plan enables exporting scans as USDZ and JSON for use in other tools.

  • Q: Do I need an internet connection to use this app?
