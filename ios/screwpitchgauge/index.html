Screw Pitch Gauge

Screw Pitch Gauge is an iOS app that helps you identify the pitch of screws by placing them directly on your iPhone or iPad screen. It supports both metric (mm) and inch (TPI) standards, making it a handy tool for DIYers and repair enthusiasts.
