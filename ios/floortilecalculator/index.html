Floor Tile Calculator

Floor Tile Calculator is a simple and easy-to-use calculator for estimating how many floor tiles you need for a room.

Enter your room size, tile size, waste percentage, tiles per box, and price per box, and the app automatically calculates the estimated number of tiles, boxes needed, and total cost.

You can calculate room area, tile area, total tiles, boxes needed, and estimated material cost from simple measurements.

This app is useful for DIY flooring projects, home renovation planning, checking tile quantities before shopping, estimating tile boxes, and understanding approximate floor tile costs.

Features

  • Estimate floor tiles quickly
  • Enter room length and width
  • Enter tile length and width
  • Choose room and tile units
  • Supports feet, inches, meters, and centimeters

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: What does this app calculate?
    A: The app estimates room area, tile area, total tiles needed, boxes needed, and total cost based on the room size, tile size, waste percentage, tiles per box, and price per box you enter.
  • Q: How do I calculate how many floor tiles I need?
    A: Enter the room length and width, then enter the tile length and width. The app calculates the room area and tile area, then estimates the number of tiles needed.
  • Q: What is waste percentage?
    A: Waste percentage adds extra tiles for cuts, mistakes, breakage, and spare pieces. For example, entering 10% adds 10% more tile coverage to the estimate.
  • Q: Can I calculate how many boxes of tiles I need?
    A: Yes. Enter the number of tiles per box, and the app estimates how many boxes you need to buy.
  • Q: Can I estimate the total cost?
    A: Yes. Enter the price per box, and the app calculates the estimated total cost based on the number of boxes needed.
  • Q: Which units are supported?
    A: The app supports feet, inches, meters, and centimeters for both room dimensions and tile dimensions.
  • Q: Can I use different units for the room and tiles?
    A: Yes. For example, you can enter the room size in feet and the tile size in inches.
  • Q: Does this app support multiple rooms?
    A: No. This app is designed for simple single-room floor tile estimates.
  • Q: Is this an official construction estimate?
    A: No. This app provides a simple estimate for planning purposes. Actual tile needs may vary depending on room shape, layout pattern, grout spacing, cuts, breakage, and installation conditions.
  • Q: Does this app include grout or adhesive calculations?
    A: No. This app focuses on estimating floor tiles, boxes, and total tile cost. It does not calculate grout, adhesive, labor, or other installation materials.
  • Q: Can I remove ads?
    A: Yes. You can remove banner ads with an optional in-app purchase.
  • Q: Do I need an internet connection?
    A: The tile calculation itself works offline. An internet connection may be required to load ads or complete in-app purchases.
