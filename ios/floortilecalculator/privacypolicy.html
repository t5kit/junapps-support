📄 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application “Floor Tile Calculator” provided by the developer.

1. Information We Collect

Floor Tile Calculator does not require account registration and does not ask users to enter personal information.

The app allows users to enter room length, room width, tile length, tile width, waste percentage, tiles per box, and price per box in order to estimate room area, tile area, total tiles needed, boxes needed, and total cost. These values are used only for calculations within the app and are not sent to the developer.

The app does not collect, store, or share any room dimensions, tile dimensions, waste percentage values, box quantity values, price values, calculation results, or usage data on external servers operated by the developer.

2. Local App Data

Floor Tile Calculator may store simple app data locally on the user’s device, such as whether ads have been removed through an in-app purchase.

Room size inputs, tile size inputs, waste percentage inputs, box quantity inputs, price inputs, and calculation results are used only to provide app functionality. They are not transmitted to the developer.

3. In-App Purchases

Floor Tile Calculator offers optional in-app purchases, such as removing ads or supporting future updates.

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive or store users’ payment card information.

4. Advertising

Floor Tile Calculator may display banner ads using Google AdMob.

Third-party advertising services may collect and use certain information, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, and ad interaction data, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Users may remove banner ads through an optional in-app purchase.

5. Third-Party Services

Floor Tile Calculator uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob for displaying ads
  • Apple App Store for processing in-app purchases

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies and platform rules.

6. Children’s Privacy

Floor Tile Calculator is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer so that appropriate action can be taken.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through the app or via the distribution platform.
