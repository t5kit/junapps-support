Margin Calculator

Margin Calculator is a simple and fast app for calculating profit, margin, markup, and selling prices.

Enter your cost and selling price to instantly see your profit, margin percentage, and markup percentage. You can also include optional selling fees and tax to get a more realistic profit calculation.

If you have a target margin in mind, enter your desired margin percentage to calculate the required selling price.

Features

  • Calculate profit instantly
  • See margin percentage
  • See markup percentage
  • Enter cost and selling price
  • Add optional selling fee percentage
  • Add optional tax percentage
  • Calculate required selling price from target margin
  • Clear inputs quickly and start again
  • Simple and clean design
  • Optional ad removal

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: What can I calculate with this app?
    A: You can calculate profit, margin percentage, markup percentage, and the required selling price for a target margin.
  • Q: Can I enter my own cost and selling price?
    A: Yes. You can enter any cost and selling price manually.
  • Q: Does the app support selling fees?
    A: Yes. You can enter an optional selling fee percentage to account for platform or payment fees.
  • Q: Can I include tax in the calculation?
    A: Yes. You can enter an optional tax percentage when needed.
  • Q: Can the app calculate a selling price from a target margin?
    A: Yes. Enter your cost, optional fee and tax, and desired target margin to calculate the required selling price.
  • Q: Does the app support different currencies?
    A: Yes. The app uses your device’s current locale to display the appropriate currency.
  • Q: Can I remove ads?
    A: Yes. You can remove banner ads with an optional in-app purchase.
  • Q: Do I need an internet connection?
    A: The margin calculation itself works offline. An internet connection may be required to load ads or complete in-app purchases.
