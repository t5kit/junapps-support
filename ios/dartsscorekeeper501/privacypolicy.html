📄 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application “Darts Scorekeeper 501” provided by the developer.

1. Information We Collect

Darts Scorekeeper 501 does not require account registration and does not ask users to enter personal information.

The app allows users to select a game mode, enter dart scores, track player scores, calculate turn totals, handle busts, and view recent turn history for casual 301 and 501 darts games. These values are used only for scorekeeping within the app and are not sent to the developer.

The app does not collect, store, or share any dart scores, player scores, game history, calculation results, or usage data on external servers operated by the developer.

2. Local App Data

Darts Scorekeeper 501 may store simple app data locally on the user’s device, such as whether ads have been removed through an in-app purchase.

Game scores and recent turn history may be kept temporarily within the app while the user is playing. This data is used only to provide app functionality and is not transmitted to the developer.

3. In-App Purchases

Darts Scorekeeper 501 offers optional in-app purchases, such as removing ads or supporting future updates.

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive or store users’ payment card information.

4. Advertising

Darts Scorekeeper 501 may display banner ads using Google AdMob.

Third-party advertising services may collect and use certain information, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, and ad interaction data, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Users may remove banner ads through an optional in-app purchase.

5. Third-Party Services

Darts Scorekeeper 501 uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob for displaying ads
  • Apple App Store for processing in-app purchases

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies and platform rules.

6. Children’s Privacy

Darts Scorekeeper 501 is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer so that appropriate action can be taken.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through the app or via the distribution platform.
