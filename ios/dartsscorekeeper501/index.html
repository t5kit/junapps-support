Darts Scorekeeper 501

Darts Scorekeeper 501 is a simple and easy-to-use score counter for casual 301 and 501 darts games.

Choose 301 or 501, tap each dart score, and submit the turn total. The app helps you keep track of scores without mental math.

This app is useful for playing darts at home, at a pub, or anywhere without an electronic dartboard or built-in score display.

Features

  • Keep score for 301 and 501 darts games
  • Simple two-player scorekeeping
  • Tap Single, Double, or Triple scores
  • Enter numbers from 1 to 20
  • Supports Bull 25 and Bull 50
  • Automatically calculates each turn total
  • Submit scores quickly after each turn
  • Handles bust turns
  • Undo or clear the current darts
  • View recent turn history
  • Switch between 301 and 501 games
  • Simple and clean design
  • Optional ad removal

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: What games does this app support?
    A: The app supports casual 301 and 501 darts scorekeeping.
  • Q: How do I enter a score?
    A: Select Single, Double, or Triple, then tap the number you hit. You can enter up to three darts per turn and submit the total.
  • Q: Does the app support bullseye scores?
    A: Yes. The app supports Bull 25 and Bull 50.
  • Q: Does the app calculate the total for my three darts?
    A: Yes. The app automatically adds your dart scores and shows the turn total before you submit it.
  • Q: What happens if I bust?
    A: If your score goes below zero, the turn is treated as a bust and your score stays the same.
  • Q: Can I undo a mistake?
    A: Yes. You can undo the current dart input or clear the current turn before submitting.
  • Q: Can I see previous turns?
    A: Yes. You can view recent turn history from the History screen.
  • Q: Can I remove ads?
    A: Yes. You can remove banner ads with an optional in-app purchase.
  • Q: Do I need an internet connection?
    A: The scorekeeping itself works offline. An internet connection may be required to load ads or complete in-app purchases.
