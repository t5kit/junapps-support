Flag & Capital Quiz

Flag & Capital Quiz is a fun and educational iOS app that lets you learn world flags, countries, and capitals through interactive quizzes — and stay motivated by collecting beautiful gems as rewards!

🌍 App Description

Explore the world and test your geography knowledge with Flag & Capital Quiz! The app features three unique modes to make learning fun and engaging:

  • 🏳️‍🌈 Flag → Country Quiz: Guess the country from its flag.
  • 🌐 Country → Flag Quiz: Choose the correct flag for each country.
  • 🏙️ Capital Quiz: Match each country with its capital city.

Every correct answer earns you a shiny gem — from crystals to crowns — that you can collect as you master all the nations! Your progress and accuracy are tracked with colorful visuals and level images that change as you improve. Whether you're a student, traveler, or trivia lover, you’ll enjoy discovering and remembering every corner of the world.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: Is this app free to use?
    A: Yes. Flag & Capital Quiz is free to download and play. Small banner ads are displayed at the bottom of the screen.
  • Q: How do I track my progress?
    A: Your effort level and correct answers are shown in a visual record screen. You can also reset your data at any time.
  • Q: Can I play offline?
    A: Yes! Once installed, you can enjoy the quizzes offline — perfect for travel or study breaks.
  • Q: Who is this app for?
    A: Anyone who loves geography! From students to adults, this app makes learning about world countries easy, fun, and rewarding.
