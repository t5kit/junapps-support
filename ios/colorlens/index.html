Color Lens – Color Assist (iOS)

Color Lens is an iOS app that helps you explore how colors appear under different visual filters using your iPhone camera.

📋 App Description

Color Lens applies real-time color filters through your camera to simulate different ways colors may be perceived. This is useful for checking packaging, art, or everyday scenes where color differences matter. Simply point your camera and observe the filtered view—no photos are saved or uploaded.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: Are photos taken with the camera saved?
    A: No. The app does not capture or store any images. The camera view is used only for real-time color visualization.
  • Q: Why does the app request access to the camera?
    A: To display a real-time filtered view using your camera. No media is stored or accessed beyond live preview.
  • Q: Does the app require an internet connection?
    A: No. All features work entirely offline.
