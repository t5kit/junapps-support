Tip Calculator

Tip Calculator is a simple and fast app for calculating tips, splitting bills, and rounding each person’s share. Enter the bill amount, choose a tip percentage, and see the total instantly.

💵 App Description

Tip Calculator is designed to make dining out easier. Quickly calculate the tip, total amount, and amount per person with a clean and simple interface.

You can also round each person’s share up or down to a clean amount. The app automatically shows the adjusted total and the effective tip percentage, so you can easily decide how much each person should pay.

✨ Features

  • Calculate tips instantly
  • Split the bill by number of people
  • Round each person’s share up or down
  • See the adjusted total after rounding
  • See the effective tip percentage
  • Simple and clean design
  • Optional ad removal

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: Can I split the bill with multiple people?
    A: Yes. You can choose the number of people and instantly see the amount per person.
  • Q: Can I change the tip percentage?
    A: Yes. You can use quick tip buttons or adjust the tip percentage manually.
  • Q: What does “Round Per Person” mean?
    A: It lets you round each person’s share up or down to a cleaner amount. The app then shows the adjusted total and effective tip percentage.
  • Q: Does the app support different currencies?
    A: Yes. The app uses your device’s current locale to display the appropriate currency.
  • Q: Can I remove ads?
    A: Yes. You can remove banner ads with an optional in-app purchase.
  • Q: Do I need an internet connection?
    A: The tip calculation itself works offline. An internet connection may be required to load ads or complete in-app purchases.
