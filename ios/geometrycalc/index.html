GeometryCalc

GeometryCalc is an intuitive geometry calculator that helps you solve shapes instantly — from triangles to cylinders — with clean visuals and accurate results.

📋 App Description

GeometryCalc makes geometric calculations fast and effortless. Enter any known values, and the app instantly calculates unknown sides, angles, areas, or volumes. It supports 15 different shapes, including triangles, circles, prisms, cones, and more — perfect for students, engineers, architects, or anyone working with measurements. Each result updates in real time with a clear layout and unit-friendly precision control. You can also save your results by project or part name, and review them anytime.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: Is this app free to use?
    A: Yes. GeometryCalc is free to download and use. You can remove ads and unlock all features with an optional subscription.
  • Q: What shapes are supported?
    A: GeometryCalc supports 15 common shapes — including rectangles, triangles, cylinders, cones, prisms, pyramids, and spheres. Each includes automatic formulas for surface area and volume.
  • Q: Can I save my calculations?
    A: Yes! You can save each calculation with a title or category (e.g., “Roof Slope,” “Beam Angle,” “Room Volume”) and access them anytime from the history screen.
  • Q: Who is this app for?
    A: GeometryCalc is useful for students, teachers, engineers, architects, metalworkers, and anyone who needs quick, reliable geometric results.
