Caliper & Ruler

Caliper & Ruler is an iOS app that allows you to measure lengths and distances using your iPhone or iPad screen.

The app transforms your device screen into a digital caliper and ruler. You can measure objects directly on the screen in real-time by dragging an intuitive knob, or use it as a standard ruler. It supports both millimeters and inches for versatile measurements.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: How do I calibrate the app for accuracy?
    A: The app includes a calibration feature that allows you to use common items like a credit card or an A4 paper sheet to ensure precise measurements.
  • Q: Is the app completely free? Are there any in-app purchases?
    A: Yes, the app is completely free to use. There are no in-app purchases or subscriptions. It is supported by small, unobtrusive ads displayed only at the bottom of the screen.
  • Q: What device orientations are supported?
    A: For iPhones, the app supports landscape orientation only. For iPads, it supports all orientations (portrait, upside down, landscape left, landscape right).
