📄 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application “Interview Prep: Practice” provided by the developer.

1. Information We Collect

Interview Prep: Practice does not require account registration and does not ask users to enter personal information.

The app allows users to browse interview questions, save favorite questions, write answer notes, use a practice timer, and track interview preparation checklist items. These values are used only for app functionality within the app and are not sent to the developer.

The app does not collect, store, or share any answer notes, favorite questions, checklist status, practice activity, or interview preparation data on external servers operated by the developer.

2. Local App Data

Interview Prep: Practice stores app data locally on the user’s device, including favorite question selections, answer notes, and checklist status.

This local data is used to display saved questions, personal answer notes, and completed checklist items. The developer does not receive or store this data on external servers.

Interview Prep: Practice may also store simple app data locally on the user’s device, such as whether ads have been removed through an in-app purchase.

3. Answer Notes and Checklist

Interview Prep: Practice allows users to write personal answer notes for interview questions and mark checklist items as completed.

Answer notes and checklist data are stored locally on the user’s device. The developer does not automatically receive, access, or review the content entered by users.

4. In-App Purchases

Interview Prep: Practice offers optional in-app purchases, such as removing ads or supporting future updates.

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive or store users’ payment card information.

5. Advertising

Interview Prep: Practice may display banner ads using Google AdMob.

Third-party advertising services may collect and use certain information, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, and ad interaction data, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Users may remove banner ads through an optional in-app purchase.

6. Third-Party Services

Interview Prep: Practice uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob for displaying ads
  • Apple App Store for processing in-app purchases

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies and platform rules.

7. Children’s Privacy

Interview Prep: Practice is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer so that appropriate action can be taken.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through the app or via the distribution platform.
