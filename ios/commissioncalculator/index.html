Commission Calculator

Commission Calculator is a simple and fast app for calculating sales commission, commission rates, and total earnings.

Enter your sales amount and commission rate to instantly see your commission amount. You can also add base pay to calculate your total earnings.

This app is useful for sales reps, freelancers, agents, and anyone who needs a quick commission or earnings calculation.

Features

  • Calculate commission instantly
  • Enter sales amount and commission rate
  • See commission amount
  • Add optional base pay
  • Calculate total earnings
  • Useful for sales, bonuses, fees, and income estimates
  • Clear inputs quickly and start again
  • Simple and clean design
  • Optional ad removal

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: What can I calculate with this app?
    A: You can calculate commission amount and total earnings from a sales amount, commission rate, and optional base pay.
  • Q: Can I enter my own sales amount and commission rate?
    A: Yes. You can enter any sales amount and commission rate manually.
  • Q: Does the app support base pay?
    A: Yes. You can enter optional base pay to calculate your total earnings.
  • Q: Can I use this for sales bonuses or fees?
    A: Yes. The app can be used for simple sales commission, bonus, fee, and income estimate calculations.
  • Q: Does the app support different currencies?
    A: Yes. The app uses your device’s current locale to display the appropriate currency.
  • Q: Can I remove ads?
    A: Yes. You can remove banner ads with an optional in-app purchase.
  • Q: Do I need an internet connection?
    A: The commission calculation itself works offline. An internet connection may be required to load ads or complete in-app purchases.
